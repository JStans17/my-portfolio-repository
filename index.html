About Me

Christopher J. Pennant is a sports announcer and journalist who's been covering sports in Chicago and around the country since 2010. Christopher searches for stories beyond the box score, no matter if the subject is professional sports or pickup games.

His work has been featured on CHGO Sports and the Arizona Republic, and he's been a featured guest on WGN Chicago and a number of different sports podcasts. His ethos is that sports is less about wins and losses and more about the idea that everyone--everyone--involved is a protagonist.

A Chicagoan (until Chicago ends), Christopher is an avid proponent of 16-inch softball. He also plays jazz trombone and will fill a need for a blues vocalist.
